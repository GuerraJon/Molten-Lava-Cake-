Molten Lava Cake Ingredients:

1 stick (4 ounces) of unsalted butter
6 ounces of bittersweet chocolate (preferably Dark chocolate)
2 eggs
2 egg yolks
1/4 cup of sugar 
A pinch of salt
2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour

Molten Lava Cake Containers & Needs: 

1 Oven 
1 Stove
1 Refridgerator 
6-ounce ramekins
2 Pots
1 medium bowl
